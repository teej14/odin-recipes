Chemex Coffee

Description

How to make coffee in a Chemex Coffeemaker.

Ingredients

  • 42g Course Ground Fresh Coffee

Steps

  1. Heat water in goose neck kettle to 205Â°.
  2. Place Chemex on kitchen scale and insert filter. Tear scale and add ground coffee (total weight 42g).
  3. Pour water evenly to saturate grounds (total weight 150g).
  4. Allow coffee to bloom 30-45 seconds.
  5. Pour water evenly and slowly (total weight 450g).
  6. Allow coffee to reintegrate for 60 seconds.
  7. Pour water evenly and slowly (total weight 700g).
  8. Allow coffee to brew, approximately 4 minutes.
